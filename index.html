Welcome to Central Ohio Asatru

This is the public page for a group of folk who hold true to the beliefs of a reconstructed religion, known as Asatru, which is based on the pre-christian beliefs and world view of their Nothern European ancestors.

Here, you will find information about our close knit group of heathens, a little about what Asatru is, a few good resources for more information and an easy way to contact us, if desired.

Please take a look around and feel more than welcome to reach out to us with any questions or comments you might have.
